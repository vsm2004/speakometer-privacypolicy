Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Speak-o-Meter, we believe your voice belongs to you. Our application is built with a "Privacy-First" architecture, ensuring your speech analysis remains completely confidential.

1. 100% On-Device Processing

Unlike other voice apps, Speak-o-Meter uses local AI (TensorFlow Lite). Your audio recordings are processed entirely on your physical device. No audio data is ever uploaded to our servers or third-party cloud services.

2. Data Collection

We only collect the minimum information required for the app to function:

  • Account Information: Your name and email used for registration and syncing local progress.
  • Speech Metrics: Statistics like your "Fluency Score" and "WPM" are stored securely to help you track progress.

3. Permissions

The app requires Microphone Access solely to capture your voice for analysis. This access is only active when you tap the record button.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time through our Account Deletion Page.
